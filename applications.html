Applications

Scientific instrumentation

Murosync is motivated by timing-sensitive scientific systems: particle accelerators, distributed detectors, telescope arrays, and timing-critical DAQ.

In these installations, deterministic timing and phase coherence are required to correlate events across physical distance and independent subsystems.

Event-based / time-encoded systems

Murosync also explores timing as a data dimension in event-based systems (event-driven sensors, neuromorphic / spiking architectures, distributed sensor fusion).

The focus is on system behavior and reproducibility — not on “AI productization”.
