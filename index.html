Distributed timing and phase synchronization for complex systems.

Murosync is a research-driven engineering project exploring deterministic timing, phase alignment, and trigger coordination across physically distributed hardware systems.

In complex distributed systems, timing errors rarely appear as simple offsets. They emerge as phase drift, correlation loss, unstable triggering, and irreproducible behavior — often only visible at full system scale.

Focus areas

  • Delay measurement and compensation (including asymmetry)
  • Phase alignment and coherence across distributed nodes
  • Deterministic trigger scheduling and distribution
  • Measurement-driven system architecture exploration
Thesis: Timing is not infrastructure — it is a system parameter.
