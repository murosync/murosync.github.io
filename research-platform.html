Research Platform

Current research prototype

Murosync is developed using FPGA-based experimental platforms designed to explore timing behavior under realistic conditions.

  • round-trip delay measurement and compensation
  • phase alignment mechanisms
  • time-scheduled trigger generation
  • repeatability and long-term stability

Measurement-driven approach

Rather than optimizing for a fixed specification, the platform is used to quantify timing limits, compare synchronization models, observe system-level effects, and identify architectural sensitivities.

The platform is intentionally experimental: designed to test assumptions, quantify limits, and iterate architectures — rather than to present a finished product.
